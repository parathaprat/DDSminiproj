Detection of Stuck At 0 Fault on JK Flip-flop

Procedure

Step-1) Enter the Boolean inputs "J" and "K".
Step-2) Enter the NODE number (1-8) of the input/output wire to be declared at stuck at 0 for the corresponding inputs.
Step-3) Click the "SA0" Button to print the output value on the truth table.
Step-4) Click "Print" to get the printout of the Truth Table.
